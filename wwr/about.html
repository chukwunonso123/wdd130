History

Founded in 1995, Wild Water Rafting has been providing unforgettable white water adventures for nearly three decades. What started as a small family business with a passion for the outdoors has grown into the region's premier rafting company. Our founders, experienced river guides themselves, built this company on the principles of safety, environmental stewardship, and creating life-changing experiences for our guests. Today, we continue that legacy with our team of certified guides who share the same passion for adventure and respect for nature that started it all.
